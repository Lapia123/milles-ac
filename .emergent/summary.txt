<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user wants to build a back-office accounting software for an FX (foreign exchange) broker.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **User Roles:** Admin, Sub-admin, Accountant, and Vendor for the back office.
- **Authentication:** JWT (email/password) and Google Social Login.
- **Client Management:** Create, Read, Update, Delete (CRUD) for clients, including KYC status, MT5 Number, and CRM Customer ID.
- **Transaction Management:**
    - Track deposits, withdrawals, and transfers linked to a client.
    - Support uploading proof of payment.
    - Specify a destination: Treasury/Bank, PSP, or Vendor.
    - Multi-currency support, with all final balances tracked in USD.
- **Treasury Management:** Manage bank accounts in different currencies. Show total treasury balance in USD, transaction history, filtering, and statement downloads.
- **Accountant Dashboard:** A dedicated view for accountants to approve or reject pending client transactions, protected by a math captcha.
- **PSP (Payment Service Provider) Management:** A manual ledgering system to track PSPs, their commissions, and settlement status.
- **Vendor Portal:**
    - A separate login and filtered dashboard for vendors to manage their assigned transactions (approve/reject with captcha).
    - Admins manage vendors, including setting deposit/withdrawal commissions.
    - Admins perform manual, multi-currency settlements for vendors, recording charges and destination treasury at the time of settlement.
- **Settlement Approvals:** Settlements performed by admins must go into a pending state for a final approval step.

**User's preferred language**: English

**what currently exists?**
A full-stack application with a React frontend and a monolithic FastAPI backend using a MongoDB database.
- **Backend ():** Contains all API endpoints and data models for users, clients, transactions, treasury, PSPs, and vendors. It handles JWT/Google auth, transaction/settlement workflows, and role-based access.
- **Frontend:** A React SPA with pages for Login, Dashboard, and management of Clients, Transactions, Treasury, PSPs, and Vendors. It also includes a dedicated portal for the Vendor role.
- **Features:** Authentication, Client/Transaction CRUD, Accountant approvals (with captcha), PSP management, and a comprehensive Vendor management system are all implemented. The UI is built with TailwindCSS and Shadcn/UI components.
