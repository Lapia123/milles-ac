<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user wants to build a back-office accounting software for an FX (foreign exchange) broker named Miles Capitals.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Branding:** Project name is Miles Capitals Back office with a white and dark blue color scheme.
- **User Roles:** Admin, Sub-admin, Accountant, and Vendor for the back office.
- **Authentication:** JWT (email/password) and Google Social Login.
- **Client Management:** CRUD for clients, including KYC status, MT5 Number, CRM Customer ID, and saved bank account details.
- **Transaction Management:** Track deposits, withdrawals, and transfers linked to a client, with proof of payment uploads. Support for multiple currencies and destinations (Treasury, PSP, Vendor, Client Bank, USDT).
- **Treasury Management:** Manage bank/USDT accounts, view total balance in USD, transaction history, and perform inter-treasury transfers.
- **Accountant Dashboard:** Approve/reject pending client transactions and settlements, protected by a math captcha.
- **PSP Management:** A manual ledger for Payment Service Providers.
- **Vendor Portal:** A separate portal for vendors to manage their assigned transactions.
- **Settlement Approvals:** Admin-initiated settlements for vendors require final approval from an accountant.
- **Income & Expenses Ledger:** A dedicated area to log company income and expenses, with custom categories. Entries should be linked to treasury accounts to automatically update balances.
- **Loan Management:** A module to track loans given to other companies, including details like borrower, amount, interest rate, dates, and repayment status. It should deduct the loan amount from a selected treasury account and track repayments.
- **Reporting:** Generate reports for P&L, income/expense by category, loan balances, and repayment history.

**User's preferred language**: English
